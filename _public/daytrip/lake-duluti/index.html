Overview

Is a crater lake located near a small town of Tengeru Arumeru

This is an amazing day trip that involves tour walk across nature of Lake Duluti. Mount Kilimanjaro and Mount Meru can be easily being viewed. Also, there is canoing along the beds of the lake. Since waters depth in the middle core of the lake has high pressure. It has depth of 700m (4,230ft). The lake has small size fish that local people come do fishing at night hours. It has a thick forest that has reptiles like monitor lizard and crocodiles.

It provides 130 different species for water birds. Most common birds found in the lake are grey herons, fish eagles, cormorants, ospreys, egrets and kingfishers.
